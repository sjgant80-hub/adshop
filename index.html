The multi-brand voice kernel

One AI. N distinct voices.

Standard AI drafts sound generic because they're grounded in one voice. AdShop's kernel is a client-portfolio kernel — each client is a separate sub-kernel with its own tone, banned phrases, past reply patterns, brand codes, and stakeholder map.

When the AI drafts a reply, it looks up which client's thread it's on and grounds the draft in that specific client's kernel. The result: Foo Cosmetics feedback replies sound like Foo Cosmetics. Bar Bank replies sound like Bar Bank. Neither ever bleeds into the other.

Voice learning happens continuously. Every time you edit an AI draft before sending, the delta is fed back into the client's voice profile. After a fortnight your drafts are 90% send-as-is.
